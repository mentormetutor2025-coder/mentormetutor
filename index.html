Who We Are

Building Brighter Futures, One Learner at a Time

Mentor Me Tutor is a non-profit organisation (NPO Act, Act 71 of 1997) based at KwaZubumnandi Pre-School, V Section, Umlazi. We exist to close the academic gap for learners in Grades 4–12 who need extra support — delivering structured, interactive and affordable tutoring in Mathematics, English and isiZulu, with a strong emphasis on mentorship and building each learner's confidence for lifelong success.

  • Focused on primary & secondary school education (Grades 4 – 12)
  • Interactive and engagement-driven learning activities
  • Experienced tutors with a mentorship approach
  • Homework support, exam preparation and study skills
  • Non-discriminatory — open to every learner regardless of background
  • Governed by a democratically-elected Board of office bearers
What We Offer

Core Subjects & Support

Every session is structured, interactive and aligned to the South African curriculum to ensure real improvement.

🔢

Mathematics

From fractions and algebra in junior grades to calculus and statistics in Grade 12 — we make Maths approachable, logical and even enjoyable.

📖

English

Reading comprehension, essay writing, grammar and literature. We build confident communicators ready for assessments and beyond.

🗣️

IsiZulu

Home language and First Additional Language support — covering oral, comprehension, writing and poetry for all grade levels.

📝

Homework Support

Structured homework sessions where learners receive guidance, build good study habits and don't fall behind on daily schoolwork.

🎯

Exam Preparation

Practice papers, revision sessions and exam technique coaching to maximise every learner's potential when it matters most.

🤝

Mentorship

Our tutors don't just teach — they mentor, motivating learners to set goals, believe in themselves and realise their full potential.
